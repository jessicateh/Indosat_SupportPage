    Mobile application


    Setting up

    1. Make sure you have the required information

      Once you have been approved for an mPOS account, you will be provided with the following information:

      • Links to download the mPOS App
      • Activation Code
      • Merchant PIN
      • Staff PIN

      You will need this information before you can start using your mPOS card reader and mPOS App. If you have not received the details above, please contact support.


    2. Download the application

      As part of the starter pack you will be provided with links, depending on the kind of mobile device you will be using, to iTunes / Google Play.

      Navigate to the appropriate link with the device that you will be using to accept payments, (i.e. iTunes for any Apple Device) and download the payment App. Please ensure that you download the correct application – the incorrect app will not work with your Account.

    Activating the app

    To activate the mobile application, first make sure you are connected to the Internet, then open the app and enter the 8 character activation code.

    If activation is not successful, check you entered the code correctly and check your Internet connection. If the error persists, please contact support to check your activation code.

    If there is no issue, an activation successful message will be displayed.

    Connecting the card reader via bluetooth

    Connecting the card reader


    Before the app can be used with the PINPad Pro card reader, the mobile device needs to be paired with the reader.

    Turn on the reader by holding the power button. Wait a while as the reader boots up - this takes about 30 seconds.

    Once you see the 'Welcome' screen, you can start Bluetooth pairing.


    Bluetooth pairing

    Press and hold the 'Bluetooth' button on the reader until you hear a beep sound. The reader is now ready to pair.


    Bluetooth settings

    Open the Settings on your mobile device and go to 'Bluetooth'. Ensure the Bluetooth is set to ON in mobile device’s settings. You should see the reader name in the list of available devices. The reader is identifiable by the last 4 digits of the reader serial number.

    This reader appears in the format MP200-xxxx, where xxxx is the last 4 digits of the serial number. The 12-digit reader serial number is printed below the barcode on the back of the reader. For example, if the reader serial number is 4313455500000845, you will see MP200-0845 in the list of available devices.

    Note: Please be careful when there is more than one PINPad Pro card reader nearby. Always make sure you are attempting to connect to the correct one. Connecting the device might differs based on phone model.


    For iOS devices:

    Tap on the device name from the available Bluetooth devices.


    For Android devices:

    Turn on Bluetooth, scan and select the device name from the available Bluetooth devices.


    Your mobile device will now display a 6-digit Bluetooth pairing code.


    Enter this code on the reader and press the green button.


    Once the correct 6-digit code is entered, the reader will be paired and ready to use. You can now open the app and start accepting card payments.

    Settings

    Settings


    Settings

    Settings allows the user to configure void and settlement for this device, add staff, reset staff PINs, inject keys into the reader and update the Merchant PIN.

    Note: Some Settings may be disabled and require a Merchant PIN to access.

    If an incorrect Merchant PIN is entered 3 times, the application will be blocked. In that event, please contact support.

    Remote key injection

    Tap the “Remote key injection” button to inject PINPad Pro device with the secure key.

    Note: Make sure the mobile is connected to the reader via Bluetooth. If it is not connected, the button will be disabled.

    Manage staff PIN

    Select a staff member from the list to reset or change their Staff PIN.

    Manage staff member's staff PIN

    Tap “Reset staff PIN” to send a new staff PIN to the selected staff member's email.

    Tap “Change staff PIN” to change the selected staff member's staff PIN.

    Add staff to this device

    Tap 'Add staff to this device' and select a staff member from the list to authorise them to use this device.

    Confirm adding staff

    Enter the staff PIN for the selected staff member to complete the process of adding the staff member to the device.

    Accepting card payments

    Accepting card payments



    Preparing to accept a card payment

    Once activated and paired with the reader, the app is ready to accept payment.

    Enter the amount and any other required fields and tap the ‘Continue’ button.

    Entering your staff PIN

    When prompted by the app, enter your 6-digit Staff PIN.

    If more than one staff member is supported on the mobile device, the user can select the staff member’s name by tapping the name field and selecting the appropriate name.

    Presenting the card

    A processing screen appears on the app and a prompt appears on the PINPad Pro to ‘TAP, INSERT OR SWIPE CARD’.

    The customer should now present his or her card.

    Processing

    A ‘Processing …’ message is displayed on the PINPad Pro and in the app while the transaction is being processed.

    Note: The exact transaction flow on the app and PINPad Pro depend upon the type of card used.

    If the card requires a signature verification, a Signature screen will appear on the app. If the card requires PIN verification, the user will be prompted to enter their PIN on the reader.

    Processing time is dependent on your network connection.

    Signature verified transaction

    Signature

    Ask the customer to sign in the space provided and taps ‘Continue’. To clear a signature and sign again, tap the ‘Clear’ button.

    Signature verified transaction receipt

    If the transaction is successful, an ‘APPROVED’ message will appear on the PINPad Pro. If the transaction has not been successful, ‘DECLINED’ will appear on the PINPad Pro display.

    A receipt in the app will also display the transaction status and details.

    To send the receipt to the customer via SMS or email, tap ‘Send receipt’.

    PIN verified transaction

    PIN verified transaction

    A processing screen appears on the app and a prompt appears on the PINPad Pro for the customer to ‘ENTER PIN’.

    PIN verified transaction receipt

    If the transaction is successful, an ‘APPROVED’ message will appear on the PINPad Pro.

    If the transaction has not been successful, ‘DECLINED’ will appear on the PINPad display.

    A receipt in the app will also display the transaction status and details.

    To send the receipt to the customer via SMS or email, tap ‘Send receipt’

    Send receipt

    To send the receipt to the customer via SMS or email, tap ‘Send receipt’. If no receipt is required, tap ‘Skip receipt’.

    Confirmation message

    A confirmation message appears informing the user that the transaction has been completed and whether the receipt has been successfully sent.

    History

    History


    View transaction history

    Tap ‘History’ in the menu to view records of past transactions.

    Export transaction history

    Enter your email address and tap the ‘Send’ button. You can view the file using any software that supports CSV format.

    Reset history

    Tap the ‘Reset history’ button, a popup window will appear.

    Note: Resetting the History will clear all records on the device. You can, however, retrieve all transaction records at any time via the Merchant Portal.

    Void

    Void


    Void transaction

    Go to the History screen and search for the transaction that you want to void. Tap the ‘Void’ button to continue with the void process.

    The Void amount is filled automatically. Enter your Staff PIN when prompted.

    Void processing

    Once ‘Confirm Void’ has been tapped, the mobile device will send the transaction data for processing.

    Void approved

    A new screen will provide notice of a successful void, along with the transaction data.

    Void declined

    If void is declined, a new screen will provide notice.
